OLIGOSCAN

Advanced Mineral & Heavy Metal Analysis

What is OLIGOSCAN?

OLIGOSCAN is a cutting-edge, non-invasive biospectroscopy technology that analyzes the mineral composition and heavy metal levels in your body. This advanced diagnostic test measures over 20 essential minerals and toxic metals present in your tissues using a specialized scanning technique, providing crucial insights into your cellular health and nutritional status.

Unlike traditional blood tests that only show mineral levels in circulation, OLIGOSCAN analyzes tissue-level mineral content, offering a comprehensive assessment of your body's mineral balance and identifying potential toxic metal accumulation that could impact your health and longevity.

Key Benefits

Mineral Assessment

Comprehensive analysis of 20+ essential minerals including calcium, magnesium, zinc, and more for optimal cellular function.

Toxic Metal Detection

Identifies harmful accumulation of heavy metals like lead, mercury, and cadmium that can accelerate aging.

Nutritional Guidance

Personalized recommendations for mineral supplementation and dietary adjustments based on your specific deficiencies.

Cellular Optimization

Supports healthy enzyme function, immune response, and metabolic processes through mineral balance.

Preventive Health

Early detection of mineral imbalances and toxic exposures prevents long-term health complications.

Performance Tracking

Monitor improvements in mineral status and toxic metal elimination over time with follow-up scans.

How OLIGOSCAN Works

1

Non-Invasive Scanning

The OLIGOSCAN device uses advanced biospectroscopy technology to scan your skin tissue. The process is completely painless, quick, and non-invasive—requiring no blood draws or injections.

2

Tissue Analysis

The device analyzes the mineral and metal composition within your tissue cells, providing a snapshot of your cellular mineral status rather than just circulatory levels.

3

Detailed Report Generation

A comprehensive report is generated showing the levels of 20+ essential minerals and toxic metals, with color-coded graphs indicating optimal, low, or high levels for each element.

4

Professional Consultation

Our anti-aging specialists review your results and provide personalized recommendations for dietary changes, supplementation, and lifestyle modifications to optimize your mineral balance.

Why Mineral Balance Matters for Anti-Aging

Minerals are essential cofactors for hundreds of enzymes that regulate aging processes. Imbalances in mineral ratios can impair immune function, accelerate oxidative stress, and compromise cellular energy production. Heavy metals displace essential minerals and generate free radicals, accelerating aging at the cellular level. OLIGOSCAN helps restore this critical balance for optimal longevity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is OLIGOSCAN safe?
Yes, OLIGOSCAN is completely safe and non-invasive. It uses bio-resonance technology with no radiation exposure and has no side effects.
How often should I get tested?
Initial OLIGOSCAN assessment is recommended for baseline data. Follow-up scans after 3-6 months of supplementation or dietary changes help track progress and adjust treatment plans.
Can OLIGOSCAN replace blood tests?
OLIGOSCAN complements blood tests by measuring tissue-level minerals rather than serum levels. Both provide valuable but different information for comprehensive health assessment.
What if my mineral levels are abnormal?
Our specialists provide personalized supplementation protocols, dietary modifications, and detoxification strategies tailored to your specific imbalances.
How long do results take to show improvement?
Most individuals notice improvements in energy and well-being within 4-8 weeks. Significant mineral rebalancing typically takes 3-6 months with consistent supplementation and dietary adherence.
