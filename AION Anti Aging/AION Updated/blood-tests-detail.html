Comprehensive Blood Tests

Advanced Biochemical Analysis for Health Optimization

The Power of Comprehensive Blood Analysis

Your blood reveals the biochemical story of your health. Standard annual blood work often misses critical markers of aging, chronic disease, and declining vitality. Our comprehensive blood testing panels go far beyond routine screenings, analyzing 50+ biomarkers that predict disease risk and identify targets for anti-aging optimization.

We measure metabolic markers, inflammation indicators, hormone levels, nutrient status, organ function, and aging-related proteins—providing a complete biochemical profile to guide personalized longevity strategies.

Testing Beyond Normal Ranges

Standard "normal" ranges are based on sick populations. We optimize for "optimal" ranges—the values associated with healthy aging and longevity. Many people fall within normal but suboptimal ranges, missing crucial intervention opportunities.

The Testing Process

Pre-Test Preparation

Fasting 10-12 hours before blood draw for accurate metabolic measurements. Hydrate well the day before.

Specimen Collection

Professional phlebotomists collect blood samples using proper protocol to ensure sample integrity.

Laboratory Analysis

Samples are analyzed using CLIA-certified laboratories with state-of-the-art testing methodologies.

Results & Consultation

Comprehensive report provided within 5-7 days. In-depth consultation interprets findings and creates optimization protocols.

FAQ

How often should I retest?
Initial comprehensive testing establishes baseline. Follow-up testing after 3-6 months tracks intervention effectiveness. Annual testing maintains ongoing optimization.
Will insurance cover these tests?
Many tests may be covered with proper medical documentation. We'll discuss insurance possibilities and provide documentation for claims.
What should I do before testing?
Fast 10-12 hours before the test. Avoid strenuous exercise, alcohol, and stress the day before. Hydrate well and get adequate sleep.
How detailed is the report?
Our reports include numerical values, optimal ranges, risk assessment, and personalized interpretations by our medical specialists.
