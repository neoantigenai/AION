Redefining Aging

The Longevity Plus Program combines diagnostics, personalized therapies, and long-term support to help you maintain vitality and health as you age.

This Program Includes

  • Consultation with senior specialists and personalised planning
  • Extensive blood test panel & hormonal profiling
  • Genetic & epigenetic testing
  • Gut microbiome analysis
  • Cell scan, face scan and body composition (BCA/BMD)
  • Personalised oral supplements & longevity formulations
  • Regular follow-ups (monthly/fortnightly) for 12 months
  • Dietary & lifestyle counselling and app access
  • Repeat testing & progress tracking
  • Ongoing physician support and care coordination

Program Timeline (12 months)

1st Visit — Initial Assessment

  • Full assessment and detailed planning
  • Extensive blood tests, cell and face scans
  • Body composition and bone density analysis

2nd Visit — 3-Month Follow-Up

  • Review tests and adjust treatment plan
  • Monthly clinical follow-ups and nutrition reviews

3rd Visit — 9-Month Comprehensive Review

  • Repeat key diagnostics and in-depth review
  • Adjust long-term maintenance plan as needed

4th Visit — 12-Month Progress Review

  • Final assessment, maintenance and next steps

Basic Assessment and Services Offered

  • Extensive blood tests (home sample collection available)
  • Cell Scan & Face Scan
  • BCA — Body Composition Analysis
  • BMD — Bone Mineral Density
  • DNA & epigenetic testing (processed in partner labs)
  • Gut microbiota analysis
  • Nutritionist, dermatologist and specialist consultations
  • Personalised supplements and diet planning
