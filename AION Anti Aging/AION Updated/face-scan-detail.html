Face Scan Analysis

Advanced Skin Analysis for Anti-Aging Assessment

Understanding Skin Aging Markers

Your face tells the story of your aging. Fine lines, wrinkles, pigmentation, elasticity, and hydration levels are visible markers of cellular aging, sun damage, oxidative stress, and internal health. Our advanced face scan technology uses AI-powered analysis to objectively measure these aging parameters and track them over time.

Unlike subjective visual assessments, our face scan provides quantifiable data on skin hydration, elasticity, pigmentation, pore size, and texture, giving you concrete baseline measurements to track the effectiveness of anti-aging interventions.

What We Analyze

Hydration Levels

Measures skin moisture content indicating barrier health and cellular water retention capacity.

Elasticity & Firmness

Assesses collagen integrity and skin resilience—key markers of youthful skin structure.

Pigmentation & Tone

Identifies sun damage, age spots, and uneven skin tone indicating UV-induced aging.

Wrinkles & Lines

Measures fine lines, wrinkles, and their depth—objective markers of skin aging.

Pore Analysis

Evaluates pore size and appearance indicating skin health and collagen degradation.

Texture & Smoothness

Surface analysis providing insight into cellular turnover and skin condition.

The Scanning Process

1

Baseline Preparation

Remove makeup and cleanse skin. The scan captures your skin in its natural state without cosmetics.

2

AI-Powered Imaging

Advanced 3D imaging technology captures detailed photographs of your facial skin from multiple angles.

3

Comprehensive Analysis

AI algorithms analyze the images, measuring hydration, elasticity, pigmentation, wrinkles, and other aging markers.

4

Detailed Report & Tracking

Results include numerical measurements, visual heat maps, and comparison to age-matched populations.

Skin as a Window to Internal Aging

Skin aging is not isolated to the surface. It reflects oxidative stress, inflammation, collagen degradation, and cellular aging occurring throughout your body. Face scan results correlate with biological age and systemic health markers.

FAQ

How long does the scan take?
The face scan takes approximately 5-10 minutes, completely non-invasive with no discomfort.
How often should I rescan?
Initial scan establishes baseline. Follow-up scans at 3-6 months help track the effectiveness of skin optimization protocols.
Does makeup affect results?
Yes, makeup should be removed for accurate analysis. The technology reads actual skin characteristics underneath cosmetics.
