ENDOPAT Assessment

Evaluating Vascular Health & Endothelial Function

Your Vascular Health Matters

Endothelial dysfunction—impaired function of the cells lining your blood vessels—is one of the earliest markers of cardiovascular aging and the underlying cause of heart disease, stroke, and dementia. It precedes clinical symptoms by years, making it an ideal target for preventive intervention.

ENDOPAT is a non-invasive, FDA-approved test that measures your endothelial function by analyzing how your blood vessels respond to reactive hyperemia (increased blood flow). This single measurement provides powerful insight into your cardiovascular aging, inflammation status, and overall vascular health.

Key Measurements

Reactive Hyperemia Index

Measures endothelial vasodilation capacity—your blood vessels' ability to respond appropriately to increased demand.

Augmentation Index

Assesses arterial stiffness and elasticity indicating vascular aging and atherosclerosis progression.

Heart Rate Variability

Analyzes autonomic nervous system balance—indicator of stress resilience and cardiovascular regulation.

Cardiovascular Risk Score

Comprehensive risk assessment based on endothelial function and vascular parameters.

Cognitive Risk Indicators

Endothelial dysfunction predicts cognitive decline and dementia risk years before symptoms appear.

Treatment Response Tracking

Monitor how lifestyle changes and interventions improve your vascular function over time.

The Testing Process

1

Baseline Measurements

Resting blood pressure and heart rate are recorded in a relaxed state.

2

Probe Placement

Non-invasive probes are placed on your fingertip and upper arm to measure arterial pulse waves.

3

Reactive Hyperemia Induction

A blood pressure cuff is briefly inflated to occlude blood flow, then rapidly released to induce a reactive hyperemia response.

4

Data Analysis & Report

Advanced algorithms analyze pulse wave responses, calculating endothelial function indices and cardiovascular risk scores.

Early Detection Saves Lives

Many people with severe endothelial dysfunction have no symptoms. ENDOPAT detects this silent killer decades before heart attacks or strokes occur, allowing time for preventive intervention to restore vascular health.

Vascular Restoration Strategies

Nutritional Support

Nitric oxide boosters, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory nutrients to restore endothelial function.

Exercise Therapy

Cardiovascular training enhances endothelial function and arterial flexibility.

Stress Management

Chronic stress damages endothelial cells. Meditation and relaxation restore vascular function.

Lifestyle Optimization

Sleep quality, smoking cessation, and dietary improvements dramatically improve vascular health.

FAQ

Is ENDOPAT testing painful?
No, ENDOPAT is completely painless. You may feel slight pressure from the blood pressure cuff, but the test causes no discomfort.
What do the results mean?
Results are compared to age and gender-matched healthy populations. Higher scores indicate better endothelial function and lower cardiovascular risk.
Can I improve my score?
Yes, lifestyle interventions can significantly improve endothelial function within weeks to months. Follow-up testing shows your progress.
