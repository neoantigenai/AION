    THE PHILOSOPHY

    Reactive vs. Proactive

    Traditional healthcare is designed to treat sickness after it happens. We are designed to prevent it.

    The median age for the onset of chronic disease is 45. But the biological processes start decades earlier. By the time symptoms appear, the damage is often done.

    AION flips the script. We use advanced diagnostics to detect early warning signs and intervene before disease strikes, adding quality years to your life.

    Common Questions

    Is this covered by insurance?

    Our memberships are designed as direct-primary care or specialty longevity services. While some diagnostic labs may be covered by insurance, the membership fee is typically out-of-pocket. We accept HSA/FSA.

    How do I get started?

    Click "Join Now" to schedule your initial discovery call. We will review your history and recommend the best tier for your needs.

    Can I upgrade my tier later?

    Absolutely. You can upgrade from Foundation to Core or Limitless at any time to access more advanced diagnostics and coaching.

    Is this only for older adults?

    No. Longevity starts now. Our members range from 25 to 85. The earlier you have data, the better you can optimize your long-term health trajectory.
